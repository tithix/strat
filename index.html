📋 Weekly Trading Plan

Monday - Observation Day

  • No trading positions
  • Establish reference candle
  • Mark key levels (PDH/PDL)
  • Prepare for Tuesday analysis

Tuesday - Reference Day

  • Analyze candle interaction with Monday
  • Determine preliminary bias
  • Watch for liquidity sweeps
  • Avoid entering trades

Wednesday - Trading Day

  • Confirm weekly bias
  • Look for high-probability setups
  • Enter trades with proper risk management
  • Monitor key level interactions

Thursday-Friday - Management

  • Manage existing positions
  • Trail stops if profitable
  • Prepare for next week analysis
  • Review weekly performance
